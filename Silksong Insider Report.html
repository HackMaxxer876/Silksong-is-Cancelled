According to Australian insiders, the highly awaited Hollow Knight: Silksong has been cancelled, and the developers, Team Cherry, have been bought by Electronic Arts.

The developers ran into several issues in developement, such as scope creep, personal issues, and lack of any concrete progress since the beginning of 2023.

The aforementioned insiders claim that Team Cherry will be releasing an official update concerning the game's cancellation and related information near the end of this month.
